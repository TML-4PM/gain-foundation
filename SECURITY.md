# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The GAIN Foundation takes security seriously. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings.

### How to Report

**Email**: security@gainfoundation.org

Please include:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Any suggested remediation

### What to Expect

- **Initial Response**: Within 48 hours
- **Status Update**: Within 7 days
- **Resolution Timeline**: Depends on severity, typically 30-90 days

### Our Commitment

- We will not pursue legal action against good-faith security researchers
- We will acknowledge your contribution (with your permission)
- We will keep you informed of our progress

### Scope

This policy applies to:
- gainfoundation.org
- All GAIN web applications
- GAIN APIs
- This GitHub repository

### Out of Scope

- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks
- Denial of service attacks
- Third-party services we use

### Safe Harbor

We consider security research conducted under this policy to be:
- Authorized under the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)
- Exempt from DMCA restrictions
- Lawful, helpful, and conducted in good faith

We will not initiate legal action against you if you comply with this policy.

## Security Measures

### Data Protection
- TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
- AES-256 encryption at rest
- No plaintext storage of sensitive data

### Access Control
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication available
- Session timeout controls

### Infrastructure
- Hosted on enterprise-grade cloud platforms
- Regular security updates
- Automated vulnerability scanning
